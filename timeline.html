• Picking Up Your First Stock


    Step-1 Have a glance on comapny's track record
    Before You Make Up Your Mind And Invest In A Company,always check the comapny's previous track record, see if it has gone through a bad phase in recent years.
  • Step-2 Always look for low debt companies
    Always have a slight glance on the companie financial status, always go for the companies which are low on debts or better in no debts at all.
  • Step 3- Sales Profit and growth
    Going through company's profit ratio and growth chart is always a better practice.
  • Step 4- Return on capital
    Check what are the dividends a company is providing to it's investors on profit.
  • Step 5-Customer Satisfaction
    Most important piece of advice, always go for the companies whose products you use in daily life and are satisfied with it, that companies are likely to generate profit. .
